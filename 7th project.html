Coding

Programming involves tasks such as: analysis, generating algorithms, accuracy and resource consumption, in a chosen programming language (commonly referred to as coding).

Design

Web design is the design of websites that are displayed on the internet and usually refers to the user experience aspects of website development rather than software development.

Launch

Launching a website is what you need to do after you've designed and built a website (on your computer), and before you tell the world about your internet-accomplishments.
